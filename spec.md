# Characters

Instead of copyrighted characters, create **original archetypes inspired by classic fantasy, anime, and action-game weapon users**. Each should have a distinct silhouette, lore, animations, and abilities.

Example archetypes:

* The Crimson Demon Swordsman (fast, stylish dual-sword fighter)
* The Shadow Blade Wanderer (high mobility katana user)
* The Storm Ronin (lightning-infused longsword)
* The Holy Knight Commander (greatsword and defensive play)
* The Abyss Reaper (cursed blade with life-steal)
* The Arcane Spellblade (magic + sword hybrid)
* The Dragon Slayer (massive weapon with super armor)
* The Celestial Samurai (precision and counters)
* The Phantom Assassin (teleports and daggers)
* The Beast Hunter (transforming weapon)

Each character should include:

* unique moveset
* ultimate
* passive
* strengths
* weaknesses
* combo routes
* lore
* personality
* visual design direction

Do not copy names, appearances, moves, dialogue, or signature attacks from existing franchises.
